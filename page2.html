Diet to gain weight/muscle

being underweight is about as good as being overweight, so this suggested diet list would help you put on a few pounds of weight after you're done with it

  • Milk

    provides a good balance of proteins, carbs, fats and a good source of calcium

    milk smoothies are a delicious way to add milk to your diet. Here is a sample recipie

    blend 1 cup of frozen berries, 1 cup of whole milk, 2 teaspoons of honey and 1 teaspoon of vanilla.

    try drinking 1 or 2 glasses of whole milk as a snack, with a meal, before or after a workout.

  • Nut Butters

    Nut butters is a huge source of calories, especially if you are consuming alot of it

    Nut butters can be added to a variety foods to turn them into a high calorie snack. Some examples includes smoothies, yogurt, crackers, etc.

  • potatoes and starch

    potatoes are a easy and cost effective way to gain extra calories.

    potatoes and other starches can not only help you gain weight, but also increases your muscle glycogen stores, which is a predominant fuel source for most sports and activities.

    types of potatoes and starch recommended

    • oats
    • corn
    • buckwheat
    • squash

  • Salmon and oily fish

    As an excellent source of protein, salmon provides many nutrients and asignificant amount of calories

    Cost wise, fresh or frozen salmon is slightly more expensive, but canned salmon is the quicker and cheaper option

    Additionally, salmon can be prepared in various ways, be it steamed, smoked, grilled, baked, or even eaten raw as Sashimi,
