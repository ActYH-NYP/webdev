keto diet

a diet for losing weight, keto diets allows one to gain more calories from protein and fat and less from carbohydrates. by eating less than 50 gramms of carbs a day, the body would run out of glucose to use which causes it to break down fats within the body and cause the person to lose weight

  • Cheese

    according to a study published in october 2018 on more than 2500 men who reported their intake of daily products, consuming more cheese is associated with a lower BMI after a 5 year follow up

    some types of cheese we recommend:

    • Goat Cheese
    • Blue Cheese
    • Parmesan Cheese

  • Meat and Poultry

    for the keto diet, meat is the main source of calories

    some types of meat we recommend

    • unprocessed bacon
    • chicken thighs
    • fatty cuts of steak

  • Seafood

    Seafood, having high protein and low carbs, is perfect for the keto diet

    some types of seafood we recommend:

    • Salmon, packed with valuable nutrients
    • Mackerel, like salmon but has a milder flavour
    • Oysters, meeting your body's needs for Zinc and Copper along with Vitamin B12
